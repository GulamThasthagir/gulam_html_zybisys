The New College is a government-aided, Autonomous, Arts and Science college affiliated to the University of Madras. It was established in 1951 by the Muslim Association of Southern India (MEASI). The college is re-accredited by NAAC with A++ (CGPA 3.61 out of 4) in the fourth cycle.
The college envisions to promote educational and economic equity by helping aspiring and underprivileged youth gain access to quality education and to nurture them into successful model citizens of the country. The vision is implemented through a 5E holistic development approach that
strives to elevate, educate, empower, engage the students and enable them to excel in life and career. The institution considers Inclusivity, Student Centredness, Excellence and Leadership Development as its core values.

The New College, Chennai Tamil Nadu was established in the year 1951 as Public (Autonomous) Educational Institute to provide higher education to build a career
in the field of Management, Humanities and Science. The New College is approved by the reputed UGC, accredited by NAAC,
and is affiliated with University of Madras, Chennai. Management of The New College, Chennai is working under Dr. S. Abdul Maliq (Principal).
